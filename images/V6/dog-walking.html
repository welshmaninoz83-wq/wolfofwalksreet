Dog Walking

Ethical dog walking in South Melbourne. Always one dog at a time, never in groups. The full session is spent walking — not being ferried around in a car.

Casual walk price: $50 for 45 minutes.

What’s included

  • 45-minute standard weekday walks (longer walks on request)
  • GPS-tracked routes and photo updates
  • Hydration checks; towel dry if wet
  • Group or solo — based on temperament & your preference

Our Commitment

  • One-on-One Walks: Your dog will never be walked in a group. 100% of our focus is on them.
  • Full Time Walking: The entire booking is spent walking — no time wasted driving dogs around.

Safety & Weather Policy

On extreme heat or severe weather days, we adjust times/routes or do enrichment visits for safety.
