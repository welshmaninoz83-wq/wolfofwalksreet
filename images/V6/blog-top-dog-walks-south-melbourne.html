Top 5 Dog-Friendly Walks in South Melbourne

Guide • 5 min read

  1. Albert Park Lake Loop — wide paths, water stops; go early on hot days.
  2. St Vincent Gardens — leafy streets and shaded loops.
  3. Westgate Park Trails — quiet tracks; keep leads on around birds.
  4. Port Melbourne Foreshore — breezy seaside strolls; check local off-lead zones.
  5. South Melbourne Market to Parks — fun urban sniffari via pocket parks.

Safety tips

  • Carry water on warm days
  • Hot pavement check (back of hand test)
  • Reflective gear for dusk
