Weekend Woof Club

Calm, supervised weekend boarding — always one dog at a time. No crowded environments, no car-ferrying. Your dog’s routine, our full attention, daily photo updates.

What to Expect

  • One Dog at a Time: Individual stays only — a calm, low-stress environment.
  • Supervised Care: Indoor/outdoor play & structured rest, tailored to your pup.
  • Daily Updates: Photos and notes so you can relax while you’re away.
  • No Car-Ferrying: Time is spent caring for your dog, not driving them around.

Feeding

Bring your dog’s usual food if you prefer, or we can supply high-quality kibble and cooked chicken at no extra charge.

Trial Visit (Video)

Before the first stay, we can do a short video call tour to show the play/rest areas and answer questions.
