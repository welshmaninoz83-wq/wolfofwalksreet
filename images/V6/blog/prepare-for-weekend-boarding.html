How to Prepare Your Dog for Weekend Boarding

Checklist • 4 min read

Quick checklist

  • Pack food + written feeding instructions
  • Bring a familiar blanket or toy
  • Confirm medication and dosages
  • Share your vet details and emergency contact

Bonus: a short pre-stay visit helps nervous pups settle quickly.
