Frequently Asked Questions

Do you walk multiple dogs?

No. We always walk one dog at a time. The entire 45 minutes is dedicated to your dog.

What times are walks available?

Weekdays only, between 7am and 7pm. All bookings must be made via the calendar system and are subject to availability.

Do you provide food during boarding?

Yes, we can feed your dog. We supply high-quality kibble and cooked chicken, or we can use your own food if you prefer.
