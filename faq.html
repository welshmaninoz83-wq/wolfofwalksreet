Frequently Asked Questions

What if it rains?

We still walk! Light rain doesn’t stop us. For extreme weather, we’ll reschedule at no cost.

Can you feed my dog?

Yes! We can provide high-quality kibble and cooked chicken or feed your own food after walks.

Do you take multiple dogs?

No. We only ever walk one dog at a time, ensuring 100% attention and care.

How do I book?

Click “Book Now” and create an account in our Time To Pet portal.

Which areas do you service?

South Melbourne, Albert Park, Port Melbourne, and nearby suburbs.

Are you insured?

Yes, we are fully insured for professional dog walking services.
