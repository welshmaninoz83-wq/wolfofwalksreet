Top 5 Dog-Friendly Spots in South Melbourne

South Melbourne is packed with fantastic locations to walk and play with your dog. Here are our top picks:

  1. South Melbourne Beach – perfect for a sandy adventure.
  2. Albert Park – a spacious area for dogs to stretch their legs.
  3. St Vincent Gardens – leafy streets and safe paths.
  4. South Melbourne Market surrounds – dog-friendly strolls near the action.
  5. Gasworks Arts Park – a local favorite for morning walks.
